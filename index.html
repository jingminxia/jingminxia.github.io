Research Interest

 During my phd, I worked on scientific computing of elliptic partial differential equations and numerical analysis, particularly focusing on math problems arising from liquid crystals. My phd thesis entitled Computational and Analytical Aspects of Energy Minimisation Problems in Cholesteric, Ferronematic and Smectic Liquid Crystals can be found here.

Currently, I am applying mathematical modelling and scientific computing techniques in the area of oceanic problems.

I mainly code with Python and occasionally Matlab and Mathematica. I use firedrake for finite element library and defcon for bifurcation analysis.

There are some selected research interests below:
  • Partial differential equations
  • Mathematical modelling and scientific computing
  • Numerical Analysis
  • Bifurcation analysis
  • Liquid crystals
